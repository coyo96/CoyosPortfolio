About Me

Web Designer | Photoshop Aficionado | Full Stack Dev


My love for graphic design began at the age of 10. My mom's friend Mitch gifted me a CD-Rom of Photoshop one night after kung-fu class. This was a start of getting a copy every single year. That and my Pumba & Simone keyboard-typing games pointed me toward a rabbit hole that would stay with me. added a simple textarea HTML code, with a message, ontop of their feather-edged image of space. Function and art? As a creative being with no where to go, I quickly became obsessed with layering images together and dissecting other people's CSS codes. This led me to design guild layouts for my friends online after school, to nowadays helping small businesses design package labels for their hopefully businesses!

My life led me in other directions, as I battled with the urge to be an artist and my mother's hopes of me becoming a doctor. It wasn't until recent years that I decided to take a step towards actually pursuing front-end development (I mean, c'mon, open wounds give me the ick!). And now I am building my portfolio and taking into consideration how I can use my voice, art history, and dev-skills to build things to sculpt with the internet.
